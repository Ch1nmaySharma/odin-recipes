Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A quick and creamy Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz pancetta or bacon, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Combine hot pasta with bacon and garlic. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg-cheese mixture.
  5. Use pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with parsley and serve immediately.
