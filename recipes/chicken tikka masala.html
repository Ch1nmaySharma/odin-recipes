Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

A classic Indian-British curry featuring marinated, chargrilled chicken pieces simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne, and salt. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat grill or broiler. Thread chicken onto skewers and grill until juices run clear. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Saut√© onions until translucent. Add garlic and ginger; cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Add grilled chicken pieces and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
