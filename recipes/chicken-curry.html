Easy Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful and comforting Indian-style chicken curry perfect for dinner with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 pound boneless chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 cup canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add onions and cook until golden.
  2. Add garlic and ginger; saut√© briefly.
  3. Add chicken and brown slightly.
  4. Stir in spices and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomatoes and coconut milk. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
